Trivia

  • This map was one of the maps which shipped with the UT2004 demo. The other maps are AS-Convoy, CTF-BridgeOfFate, DM-Rankin and ONS-Torlan. A latter demo also added CTF-FaceClassic and ONS-Primeval.
