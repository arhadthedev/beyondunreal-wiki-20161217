we are endeavouring to build bridges between people in wiki communities.

We have 20,824 pages about wikis, wiki people and wiki ideas.

We strive to share wiki experiences, and encourage wiki folks to think about issues in the wiki community. We use structured data based on MediaWiki, the engine, of which we have lots of info about wiki software, of Wikipedia, with some differences.
