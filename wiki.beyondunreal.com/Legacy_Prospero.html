Works In Progress:[edit]

Firestarter combo/powerup[edit]

When activated anyone who looks into the player's eyes will catch on fire.

Chicken Joust[edit]

This is a new gametype where kills are scored by landing on an opponent's head or by hitting an opposing knight in the head with a lance. Players can fly around in a fashion similar to the Atari game Joust by using the jump button.
