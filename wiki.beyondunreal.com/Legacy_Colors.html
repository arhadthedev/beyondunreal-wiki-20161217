Colors[edit]

Colors are most often specified in hue, saturation, and brightness (HSB), but in some contexts in red, green, and blue (RGB).

LightBrightness = 255, LightSaturation = 0
LightHue:   0  16  32  48  64  80  96 112 128 144 160 176 192 208 224 240 255
Colour:                                  

The brightness (LightBrightness) controls the overall intensity or range of the color. The hue (LightHue) specifies the base color. The saturation (LightSaturation) specifies how much white is added to the base color.
