This is a class for the ring part of the domination control point hologram. The letters are separate, covered by xDOMLetter.

Properties[edit]

The following properties are used by xDomPoint to assign appropriate team colors.

Material RedTeamShader 
Shader to paint the ring in the red team's color.
Material BlueTeamShader 
Shader to paint the ring in the blue team's color.
Material NeutralShader 
Shader to paint the ring in neutral color.
