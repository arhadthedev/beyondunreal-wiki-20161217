ZxAnPhOrIaN: Well... this page is supposed to help people to create a button for the custom brush builders and the custom toolbox. Questions like "How did Tarquin draw the torus button for the torus builder, like shading, gradients, sides, etc. ?" should go into here.

Tarquin: ahh..... I see! Making Icons then, maybe?

The Torus button: I made a big torus with a really high detail setting (16 x 16 sides I think, maybe even 32). I found a white texture (UWindow.WhiteTexture), and make a white room, with the white torus inside. I then set up some purple lights to one side, moved the camera back and took a big screenshot in UnrealEd. Then in Photoshop I shrunk it down so it looks smooth. :)

ZxAnPhOrIaN: Is that a joke, or did you really do that?
