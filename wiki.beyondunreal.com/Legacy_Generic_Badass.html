Overview[edit]

Well, I'm pretty much just another FPS fan, born and raised in the FPS world on Doom, Quake, Quake II, and Quake III. I had heard of UT but didn't really think much of it. Then, once upon a time, I downloaded the UT demo just to see what all the hubbub was about. I liked it enough that I bought UT:GOTYE shortly thereafter.

I've been a casual mapper for Q2 (and Q3 somewhat) and I wanted to try my hand at UT mapping. UT is much different than I anticipated; much more sophisticated, inside (mapping, engine) and out (gameplay). The jury's still out on whether or not I like it more than Q3.

Contact Info[edit]

I don't use e-mail anymore unless it's absolutely necessary (eg., to sign up for something) because of spam. My preferred form of contact is via IRC or various message boards.

  • IRC: enterthegame.com, generally in #php, #maps, #higs and #ut
  • Homepage: Generic Personal Quake Site, the primary content of which are the maps I've made, most of which are for Q2. (it covers UT too; as I said, I started out with Quake)
  • In my homepage is a small message board at which you can leave messages for me if you wish.
