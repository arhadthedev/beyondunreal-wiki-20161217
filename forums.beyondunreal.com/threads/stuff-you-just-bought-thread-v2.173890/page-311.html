    Corsair CX750M, Corsair RM750x, EVGA SuperNOVA 650 G1, or ANTEC HCG-900 (I have this one) are all pretty solid and would be my suggestions. Though what you describe is also possibly a posting issue from the MB. However said posting issues could be caused by the PSU resulting in a damaged board and/or other components.

    It is important to remember that with PSU's the wattage rating is its max. You never want to max out a PSU. For most computers 500 is safe. But gaming rigs with multiple cards can get awfully close. You of course probably know this but I am gonna restate it here for anybody who reads this that doesn't.
