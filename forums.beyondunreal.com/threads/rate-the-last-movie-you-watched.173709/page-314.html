    Leto was a fairly good Joker, which may seem at odds with some who think Heath Ledger was the model to beat... but while I did enjoy Ledger's take he wasn't a good Joker.

    Ledger played an excellent crazy version of the character, but Joker is more than just crazy. Going by the comics as the canon, Joker doesn't care about chaos or anything like that. He's mostly about people knowing he did the crazy thing and fully embracing his crazy. Plus all the Joker-themed gear. When you come down to it, Jack Nicholson's Joker, while a bit too old to fight Batman on equal terms, was the most true to the comics version yet.

    Leto comes a close second for all the costuming and whatnot. When he busted in with the SWAT team gear and it said "Joker" on front instead of anything more official? I had a lil' geek-gasm!
