Lars Aronsson

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search
Lars Aronsson at Wikimania 2016

Lars Erik Aronsson (born March 19, 1966 in Örebro, Sweden) is a Swedish programmer and consultant, and the founder of two Swedish web sites: the free electronic book archive Project Runeberg and the Swedish language wiki Susning.nu.[1]

Aronsson was awarded "IP-priset" 2007 for his work with Project Runeberg, Susning.nu and Wikimedia Sverige. He has also attracted attention for his work with OpenStreetMap.[2]
