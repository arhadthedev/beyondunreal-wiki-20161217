RMusicPlayer is a new music player for UT. It's independent of your current sound driver, so it'll work with Galaxy, FMod or OpenAL. Features:

 * Plugin support
 * In theory it can support all audio files supported by FMODEx, tested file formats are:
   - FLAC
   - MP2
   - MP3
   - OGG
   - WMA
   - WAV
 * Mod support (you can subclass RMusic_Player to define new music directory) 
 * Save support in SP games
 * Crossfades/fades out/fades in music
