SMWCon, or the Semantic MediaWiki Conference, is an annual gathering for users, developers and enthusiasts of Semantic MediaWiki, including corporate users, academics and others. It is held in Europe, in the fall. From 2010 to 2015, SMWCon was a twice-annual event, also occurring in the spring in North America; in 2016, the North American event was renamed to the Enterprise MediaWiki conference, or EMWCon.

SMWCon

Upcoming

The 14th SMWCon, SMWCon Fall 2017, will be held in The Netherlands, early October 2017.

Past

Thirteen SMW Conferences have been held so far:

  • SMWCon Fall 2016 – 28 to 30 September 2016 in Frankfurt am Main, Germany
  • SMWCon Fall 2015 – 28 to 30 October 2015 in Barcelona, Spain
  • SMWCon Spring 2015 – 6 to 8 May 2015 in St. Louis, Missouri, USA
  • SMWCon Fall 2014 – 1 to 3 October 2014 in Vienna, Austria
  • SMWCon Spring 2014 – 21 to 23 May 2014 in Montreal, Canada
  • SMWCon Fall 2013 – 28 to 30 October 2013 in Berlin, Germany
  • SMWCon Spring 2013 – 20 to 22 March 2013 in New York, New York, USA
  • SMWCon Fall 2012 – 24 to 26 October 2012 in Cologne, Germany
  • SMWCon Spring 2012 – 25 to 27 April 2012 in Carlsbad, California, USA
  • SMWCon Fall 2011 – 21 to 23 September 2011 in Berlin, Germany
  • SMWCon Spring 2011 – 28 to 30 April 2011 in Washington, D.C, USA
  • SMWCon Fall 2010 – 18 and 19 September 2010 in Amsterdam, Netherlands
  • SMWCon Spring 2010 – 22 and 23 May 2010 in Boston, Massachusetts, USA

There have also been previous Semantic MediaWiki-related events under different names:

  • SMW Camp – 7 and 8 November 2009 in Karlsruhe, Germany
  • SMW Meetup – 25 March 2009 in Stanford, California, USA
  • SMW Users Meeting – 22 and 23 November 2008 in Boston, Massachusets, USA

EMWCon

EMWCon, or the Enterprise MediaWiki Conference, is an event spun off from SMWCon.

Upcoming

  • EMWCon Spring 2017 - 8 to 10 March in McLean, Virginia, USA.

Past

  • EMWCon Spring 2016 - 25 to 27 May in New York, New York, USA.
